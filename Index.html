About the LA

Los Angeles , often called "LA", is a city in California known for its Hollywood entertainment industry, iconic beaches, and warm climate; originally founded by Spanish settlers in 1781 as "El Pueblo de Nuestra Señora Reina de los Ángeles" (The Town of Our Lady Queen of the Angels), making it part of Mexico before becoming part of the United States after the Mexican-American War
